## Photography

### Style
- High contrast automotive imagery
- Dark backgrounds with dramatic lighting
- Focus on vehicles and workshop environments
- Include local Somaliland context

### Avoid
- Stock photos with watermarks
- Low-quality images
- Images that don't reflect local market

## Iconography

### Library

### Usage
- **Mono:** Single color icons
- **Contained:** Icon in colored circle
- **Interactive:** Icon buttons with hover states
